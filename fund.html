FUNDRAISE

Help us fundraise by organizing a fundraising event or campaign, such as an online crowdfunding campaign.

At Compassionate Hands Community Initiative (CHCI), we rely on the generous support of individuals, groups, and organizations to help us achieve our mission. Fundraising is a vital part of our efforts, and we invite you to join us in making a difference.

There are many ways to fundraise for CHCI:

Host an Event

Organize a local event such as a bake sale, charity run, or gala. These events not only raise funds but also build community awareness and support.

Online Fundraising

Set up an online crowdfunding campaign to reach a larger audience. Share your campaign on social media to maximize reach and impact.

Corporate Sponsorship

Partner with businesses to secure sponsorships. Corporate sponsors can provide financial support and resources to help us achieve our goals.

No matter how you choose to fundraise, your efforts make a significant difference in the lives of those we serve. Together, we can create positive change and support those in need.
